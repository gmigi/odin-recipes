Meatballs

Description

Spherical pieces of pork/beef/chicken that are browned, fried, or grilled and served as is, on bread, or with pasta dishes.

Ingredients

  • Ginger
  • Green Onion
  • Minced Meat
  • Egg
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Potato Starch
  • Sesame Oil
  • Flour
  • Dashi
  • Ketchup
  • Sugar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garnished with parsley

Steps

  1. Grate!
  2. Chop up!
  3. Weigh the meat!
  4. Add the ingredients!
  5. Knead!
  6. Roll meatballs!
  7. Coat!
  8. Deep fry!
  9. Stew!
